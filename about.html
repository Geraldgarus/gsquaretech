Who We Are

We are a software development company specializing in building custom digital solutions for businesses in Tanzania and beyond. Our expertise spans hotel management systems, point-of-sale platforms, inventory systems, professional websites, and full-scale business software — all designed to solve real problems and drive growth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help businesses move away from manual processes and embrace smart, affordable, and reliable digital tools. Whether you run a hotel, a shop, a hospital, a school, or any business — we build the software that takes your operations to the next level.

What We Do

We deliver end-to-end software solutions — from planning and design to deployment and ongoing support.

Hotel & Business Management Systems

We build full Property Management Systems (PMS) covering reservations, housekeeping, restaurant POS, inventory, maintenance, and financial reports. Our Steps Premium Suite is a live example.

Point of Sale (POS) Systems

We develop custom POS solutions for shops, supermarkets, restaurants, bars, and pharmacies — with real-time sales tracking, cashier management, and daily reporting.

Professional Websites

We design and build fast, mobile-responsive websites for hotels, businesses, schools, and personal brands — with hosting setup, domain management, and ongoing maintenance.

Inventory & Stock Management

We create inventory systems that track products, purchases, and stock levels in real time — eliminating manual counting and reducing losses.

Custom Business Software

From HR and payroll systems to hospital booking and school management — we build whatever your business needs, tailored exactly to how you work.

IT Consultancy & Network Setup

We handle network design, Wi-Fi setup, IT infrastructure planning, and security — ensuring your business technology runs smoothly and securely.
